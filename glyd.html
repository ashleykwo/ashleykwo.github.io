GLYD

Web Design

GLYD is a social media app dedicated to connecting travelers and locals. Users can share their favorite travel experiences, connect with fellow GLYDrs, and hire locals to create custom itineraries, curating a list of places to eat, see and explore for an unforgettable immersive travel experience.

Overview

During the summer of 2017, I interned at GLYD, a local travel app startup based in Los Angeles, CA to build and launch their company website.

The goal was to create a platform to promote, inform, and engage potential GLYD app users, meanwhile, providing a travel resource for sharing information about places and experiences around the globe.

Ideation

Collaborating with a UI/UX designer, we aimed to create a website with a visual feel and reinforced the app's colors of blue and white.

During the initial stages, a lot of research went into finding a WordPress theme geared toward promoting mobile apps with a masonry grid layout for the blog. Eventually, we settled on The Gem theme, because of its design, flexibility and customization options.

Note: Since its launch, the company site has undergone a total revamp.
